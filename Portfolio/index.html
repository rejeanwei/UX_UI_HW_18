About Me

Hi, I’m Rejean and I am a UX Designer .

I am a designer with a passion for helping people solve their problems. I design products that are simple, easily navigable and accessible to all.

My Skills

User research: Starting with a proto persona, I learn what sort of needs and what to prioritize for my users. From there I conduct interviews and secondary research to back up my findings.

User empathy: By emphasizing and connecting with my clients during user interviews, I apply their frustrations into changes within my projects.

Qualitative interviews: I like to approach qualitative interviews by focusing on the “why” aspect. Typically I like to ask questions such as “Why was this done this way?” to gauge the thinking behind my clients and how to approach that.

My Work

Itinereasy

A product design of a mobile app made to help people plan their trips. This was my first venture as a designer and details my thought process as a designer.

Canada Post Redesign

Tasked with redesigning a government website, I prototyped and created a new look for Canada Post. Filled with fresh colours and improved navigation, user tests found this redesign easy to use.

A Way Home Redesign

Working in a small team of 4, me and my colleagues helped redesign the website of A Way Home. Given a deadline of 3 weeks, we were able to redesign the homepage, navigation and improve the overall theming and consistency of the website.

BabyThrift

Given an objective to design a product to help people with their everyday problems, our team designed BabyThrift. BabyThrift is an app that sells maternity and baby clothing using a subscription based model. This was my first group project where our team had to display teamwork, communication and operate together in order to create a truly great project.
